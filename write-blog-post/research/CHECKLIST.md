# Pre-publish checklist

Run through this list before returning the draft to the user.

## SEO
- [ ] Target keyword appears in title, H1, first 100 words, at least one H2, and meta description.
- [ ] Slug is short, lowercase, hyphen-separated, and contains the keyword.
- [ ] Meta description is 150–160 characters and compels a click.
- [ ] At least 2 internal links to related pages (use topic clusters as the source).

## Structure
- [ ] One H1 only.
- [ ] Hierarchical H2/H3 headings, no skipped levels.
- [ ] Intro hooks the reader and states the payoff in 2–3 sentences.
- [ ] Conclusion summarizes and includes a clear call to action.

## Readability
- [ ] Paragraphs under 4 sentences.
- [ ] Sentence length varies; no run-ons.
- [ ] No filler phrases ("In today's fast-paced world...", "It goes without saying...").
- [ ] Transition words used between sections.

## Accuracy
- [ ] Every statistic, quote, and claim has a source or a `[TODO: source]` marker.
- [ ] Product names, feature names, and prices match `knowledge/` docs.
- [ ] No invented case studies or testimonials.

## Formatting
- [ ] YAML frontmatter present with title, description, keyword, slug.
- [ ] Code blocks have a language tag.
- [ ] Images (if any) have alt text.
